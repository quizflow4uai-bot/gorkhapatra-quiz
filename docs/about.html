About Samasamaik

Welcome to Samasamaik — a free educational platform dedicated to helping competitive exam aspirants in Nepal stay informed through high-quality current affairs quizzes, concise explanations, and structured learning resources.

The platform primarily transforms important current affairs published in trusted Nepali newspapers, especially Gorkhapatra, into interactive multiple-choice questions. Each quiz includes detailed explanations and key study points, making revision faster, smarter, and more effective.

Whether you are preparing for Loksewa (Public Service Commission), Nepal Rastra Bank (NRB), Banking, Teacher Service Commission (TSC), Nepal Police, Armed Police Force (APF), Nepal Army, Foreign Service, Engineering, or other competitive examinations, Samasamaik provides an efficient way to stay updated with current affairs while strengthening your exam preparation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make current affairs preparation simple, reliable, and accessible for every learner in Nepal by leveraging technology and artificial intelligence.

We believe students should spend more time understanding important events and less time searching for reliable study materials.

What We Offer

  • Daily and weekly current affairs quizzes
  • AI-assisted question generation
  • Detailed explanations and study points
  • Topic-wise categorization
  • Bookmark important questions
  • Quiz history and progress tracking
  • Mobile-friendly interface
  • Free access for all learners

Why Samasamaik?

Preparing for competitive examinations often requires reading newspapers every day, identifying important topics, and making personal notes. This process can be time-consuming.

Samasamaik simplifies this by converting significant current affairs into exam-oriented quizzes with explanations, allowing learners to revise important topics quickly and effectively. The platform is designed to save time while improving understanding and retention.
